## ADAC Ravenol 24h Nürburgring

A 24-hour endurance race on a combined layout of the Nürburgring Nordschleife and the modern Grand Prix circuit — over 25 kilometres of mixed track per lap. Held annually in late May or early June and run by the ADAC, with Ravenol as title sponsor.

Entries are split into multiple classes: top-class GT3 cars run alongside Touring Cars, Cup machinery, and amateur-friendly Cayman / Renault Clio fields. A typical grid exceeds 130 cars, with around 700 drivers competing through the night.

The event has been won by Audi, Mercedes-AMG, BMW, Porsche, Ferrari, and Lamborghini factory programmes, often by minimal margins after weather and traffic chaos. The Nordschleife's combination of length, elevation change, and unpredictable weather makes it one of the most demanding races on the calendar.

— Series colour matches ADAC's signature yellow.
